Raver · Privacy

Local first, by design.

This policy describes the current Raver: DJ & Festival Radar iOS pilot. Raver does not use advertising trackers, sell personal data, or automatically upload your local profile and event decisions to a Raver account or analytics backend.

No cross-app tracking No advertising profile No automatic decision upload

English

1. Information stored on your device

Raver stores app preferences and product state locally, including onboarding choices, selected city, saved events, followed artists, watched ticket baselines, read status, local Squad plans, and notification preferences. This data remains on the device in the current pilot and is removed when the app is deleted.

2. Public event data

The app fetches a reviewed public event feed and its publication-health report from GitHub Pages. These requests do not include your Raver profile, saved events, Squad plan, precise location, or a Raver account identifier. As with ordinary web hosting, GitHub may process standard network information such as an IP address and user agent under its own privacy terms.

3. Optional device permissions

  • Location: requested only after you use the Radar location action. Distance is calculated on the device; precise location is not added to the event feed or Squad data.
  • Calendar: write-only access is requested when you choose to add an event. Raver does not read your existing calendar.
  • Notifications: local event reminders may be scheduled on the device. The current pilot does not register a Raver push-notification token.
  • Clipboard: an assisted ticket handoff copies the displayed search term only after you tap the corresponding action.

4. Sharing you initiate

Event invitations, weekly pilot summaries, and event-correction reports are prepared locally. They leave the device only after you open the iOS share sheet and select a destination. Optional free-text notes may contain information you choose to write, so review them before sharing.

5. External services

Ticket, venue, promoter, music, map, WeChat, and other external links open services operated by third parties. Their privacy and purchase terms apply after you leave Raver. Raver does not receive payment-card details or control external ticket orders.

6. TestFlight

During beta testing, Apple may provide the developer with TestFlight crash information, usage statistics, and feedback linked to the tester account, according to Apple's TestFlight terms. This Apple beta service is separate from Raver's local decision journal.

7. Retention, deletion, and changes

The current build has no active Raver cloud account containing your local profile. Delete the app to remove its local state. If remote accounts, analytics, server-synced Squad features, advertising, or payment processing are enabled later, this policy and App Store disclosures will be updated before that release.

8. Contact

For privacy or support questions, open an issue in the Raver support tracker. Do not include sensitive personal, payment, or precise-location information in a public issue.

简体中文

1. 保存在设备上的信息

Raver 会在本机保存 App 偏好与产品状态，包括引导选择、城市、收藏活动、关注艺人、票价关注基线、已读状态、本机小队计划与通知偏好。当前试用版不会把这些数据上传到 Raver 云端；删除 App 后，本机数据会一并移除。

2. 公开活动数据

App 会从 GitHub Pages 获取经过审核的公开活动数据和发布健康报告。请求中不包含你的 Raver 资料、收藏、小队计划、精确定位或 Raver 账号标识。与普通网站托管一样，GitHub 可能依据其隐私条款处理 IP 地址、User-Agent 等标准网络信息。

3. 可选设备权限

  • 定位：只有在你使用 Radar 定位操作后才会请求。距离在本机计算，精确定位不会写入活动数据或小队数据。
  • 日历：只有当你选择把活动加入日历时，才会请求只写权限。Raver 不会读取已有日历。
  • 通知：活动提醒可在设备上本地安排。当前试用版不会注册 Raver 推送通知 token。
  • 剪贴板：只有当你点击辅助购票跳转时，才会复制页面上显示的搜索词。

4. 由你发起的分享

活动邀请、试用周报和活动纠错内容都在本机生成。只有当你打开 iOS 分享面板并选择目标后，内容才会离开设备。可选备注由你自由填写，发送前请自行检查其中是否包含个人信息。

5. 外部服务

票务、场馆、主办方、音乐、地图、微信等链接会打开第三方服务。离开 Raver 后，适用相应第三方的隐私和购买条款。Raver 不接收银行卡信息，也无法控制外部票务订单。

6. TestFlight

测试期间，Apple 可能依据 TestFlight 条款向开发者提供与测试账号关联的崩溃信息、使用统计和用户反馈。Apple 的测试服务与 Raver 本机决策记录是不同的数据路径。

7. 保留、删除与政策变更

当前版本没有启用保存本机资料的 Raver 云端账号。删除 App 即可移除本机状态。未来若开放远程账号、分析、云端小队、广告或付款处理，我们会在相应版本发布前更新本政策和 App Store 披露。

8. 联系方式

隐私或支持问题可以提交到 Raver 支持跟踪页。请勿在公开 Issue 中填写敏感个人信息、支付信息或精确位置。
